# Wird — Design Influences

| Source | Take | Leave |
|---|---|---|
| **Kalam** | Warmth in the home — personal greeting, soft colors, rounded cards. Sense of a *finished* product. Arabic-letter texture as background pattern. Generosity of space. | XP, levels, streaks, laurel crowns — vanity engine. Guilt-flame streak counter. Dense multi-door home screen. Gamified 3D level badge. |
| **Forest** | Growth as an intrinsic metaphor — the reward *is* the activity made visible. | The death mechanic — killing your tree is the same guilt-engine as a streak. |
| **LingQ / Quran.com** | Status lives at the point of reading; word audio; word-by-word glosses. | Heavy bottom sheets, source-pickers, root/lemma clutter, manual grading. |
